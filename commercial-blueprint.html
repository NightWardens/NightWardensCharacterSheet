01 / Product Strategy

What the site needs to do

The site should behave like the official access portal for a supernatural field organization: public storefront, secure archive, digital library, and companion tools hub.

Convert New Visitors

Explain the premise fast, show what to buy first, and keep the Core Rulebook path visible from every major page.

Support Pilot Buyers

Present Pilot Edition as a fully playable first field issue with update support and future upgrade eligibility.

Build Ownership

Give buyers an account, order history, downloadable files, version access, and eventually searchable owned content.

Prepare Digital Tools

Make room for the character builder, Auto-GM, bestiary, grimoire, rules archive, and Ask the Dossier assistant.

04 / Accounts + Library

Ownership is the foundation for future tools

1

Create account

Email/password or magic link at MVP. Google and 2FA can come later.

2

Buy product

Stripe checkout creates the order and triggers a library entitlement.

3

Open library

Owned books, downloads, version notes, and eligible updates appear in one place.

4

Upgrade eligibility

Pilot owners can see eligibility, proof status, and future discounted upgrade paths.

ASK THE DOSSIER

Query: “How do I prepare for a ghost case?”

Archive result: salt, iron, anchor investigation, remains or tether object. Full answer should cite owned rule sections and never invent hidden case facts.

05 / Rules Assistant

Make it feel like an archive, not a generic chatbot

The assistant should explain rules, find sections, compare roles, and summarize owned content. It should not replace the rulebook or invent official rules.

  • Use “Ask the Dossier” or “Warden Assistant” as the name.
  • Show source filters: Quickstart, owned books, cards, rules archive.
  • Show cited sections and related products beside answers.
  • Launch search first; add LLM after ownership and permissions are stable.

07 / Owner Workflow

Admin upload and sale flow

01

New product

Choose product type, title, label, price, format, and publish status.

02

Upload files

Add PDF, EPUB, ZIP, product images, print files, and version labels.

03

Stripe price

Attach or create Stripe price ID from the admin panel.

04

Library entitlement

Decide what the buyer owns after checkout: download, archive, tool access, or full.

05

Version update

Upload Pilot v1.1, mark current, add changelog, notify owners.

06

Manual grant

Search a customer and grant owned items after support requests or proof approval.
