How the Engine Thinks

Parser: turns typed text into actor, privacy, intent, target, topic, and roll profile.
Roll Engine: reads character attributes/skills, applies setbacks, support-skill offsets, and resolves result tier.
Witness + Investigation: uses witness trust/fear/secrets and evidence thresholds to reveal public or private clues.
Creature AI: uses entity type, pressure clock, tarot domain, and player failure to stalk, mislead, attack, flee, or escalate.
Shared/Private Layer: separates party-visible truth from split-player private discoveries.
