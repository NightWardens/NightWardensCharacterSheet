Core Check

Roll 3d6 ≤ Attribute + up to 2 Skills − Setbacks

Setbacks represent darkness, fear, injury, chaos, supernatural interference, bad position, or missing tools. Support skills can remove pressure when the fiction supports them.

Easy: +2
Standard: +0
Hard: -2
Extreme: -4

Round Flow

1. Intent: Say what you do, how, and which attribute applies.
2. Roll: Roll only when uncertain and failure matters.
3. React: Target may Dodge, Brace, or Counter once.
4. Damage: Roll damage/effect, then apply state.
5. Update: Injuries, afflictions, hazards, escalation.

Actions at a Glance

Major: Attack, full skill use, major inspection, rite, trap setup, treatment.
Minor: Assist, quick search, draw item, reload, mark clue, short exchange.
Move: Reposition, approach, retreat, cross room/zone.
Reaction: Dodge, Brace, Counter, protect ally, trigger prepared action.
Recovery: Stabilize, catch breath, suppress effect.

Inspections / Investigation

Ask 3: Method? Approach? Attribute?
Field: Instinct — scene, tracks, disturbances, hidden danger.
Research: Intellect — lore, records, symbols, weaknesses.
Social: Charisma — witnesses, cover identity, pressure, bargains.
Technical: Tinkering/Intellect — EMF, recordings, forensics, devices.
Full Success: remove 2 possibilities, precise clue, +1 forward. Partial: remove 1. Failure: false lead, danger, time loss, suspicion, escalation.

Quick Spell Deck

Ritual Logic

Quick Spell: 3d6 ≤ Will + Energy Control or Ritual Basics − Setbacks

Structured Rite: 3d6 ≤ Will + Ritual Casting + support skill − Setbacks

Binding / Warding: 3d6 ≤ Will + Binding or Warding − Setbacks
Summoning Frame: Core Components + Catalyst + Anchor + Cost.
Magic Risk: strain, rare materials, exposure, corruption, attention, affliction marks, or loss of control.
Fast spells buy seconds. Structured rites buy control. Forbidden workings buy answers and then charge interest.
